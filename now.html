Where Are They Now?

As often is the case with folk tradition, the existence of an entity is directly tied to the perception of it in the public's mind. Since the release of Among Us in 2018, popularity of the video game has subsided somewhat, and in turn, so have reports of the mysterious Imposter. Police Reports indicate that a person or creature resembling The Imposter has never been caught, so the question remains... if The Imposter truly was and is real, is he still out there somewhere... Among Us?
